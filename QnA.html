    Question 1: What is Student Management?

    Student Management refers to the processes and systems used to manage student data, including enrollment, attendance, grades, and communication with students and parents.

    Question 2: How do I register?

    You can register by clicking on the 'Register' link in the navigation menu and filling out the required information.

    Question 3: What features does the system offer?

    The system offers features such as student enrollment, grade tracking, attendance management, and communication tools for teachers and parents.

    Question 4: Who can access the system?

    The system can be accessed by students, teachers, and administrative staff who have registered and logged in.
